About

Hello! I'm a software engineer, teacher, and motivational speaker. I focus on helping others achieve their full potential via cognitive coaching, which combines effective learning techniques and psychology concepts.

Background

I graduated with a Bachelor's in Computer Science in 2014. I then took a year to delve into aromatherapy and holistic healing, and I have since returned to software engineering with a renewed interest in its potential to help people.

Next steps

Currently, my focus is on creating intelligent learning software, as well as applications that allow people to connect more meaningfully.

I am also interested in further developing my coaching and public speaking skills. At my current job, I lecture in front of 80 people every day, and I spend 20 hours a week mentoring junior developers.
